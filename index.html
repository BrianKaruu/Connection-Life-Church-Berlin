Welcome to Connection Life Church Berlin!

We Are So Glad You're Here!

At Connection Life Church, we believe in growing together in faith, fellowship, and service. Founded in 2016 by Bishop Sammy Irungu, our church is deeply committed to the message of the cross and the powerful truth of the risen Christ.

We offer a variety of programs to support individuals at every age and stage of life:

  • Sunday School - Engaging and interactive classes for children to learn about God's Word in a fun, safe environment.
  • Teens and Youth Church - A space for young people to connect, worship, and explore their faith.
  • Women's and Men's Fellowships - Building strong communities with regular meetings offering support, encouragement, and spiritual growth.

Whether you're looking for a place for your children or a group to connect with, we have a place for you!
