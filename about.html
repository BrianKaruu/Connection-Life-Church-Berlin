Greetings from the Bishop

Bishop Sammy Irungu

Welcome to Connection Life Church Berlin! It is my honor and privilege to serve as the founder and lead pastor of this wonderful community.

Since 2016, God has been building something beautiful here in Berlin. What started as a small gathering has grown into a thriving church family committed to the message of the cross and the transformative power of Jesus Christ.

My vision for CLC Berlin has always been simple yet profound: to create a space where people can encounter God's love, grow in their faith, and discover their purpose. We are not just building a church; we are building a family‚Äîa community of believers who support, encourage, and uplift one another.

As your pastor, my heart is to see every person who walks through our doors experience the life-changing power of the Gospel. Whether you're searching for answers, seeking healing, or desiring to grow deeper in your relationship with God, you have a home here at CLC Berlin.

Our Journey

A Story of Faith, Growth & Impact

2016
The Beginning

Connection Life Church Berlin was founded by Bishop Sammy Irungu with a vision to establish a Christ-centered community in Berlin. Starting with a small group of passionate believers, the foundation was laid for what would become a thriving church family.

2017-2019
Growth & Expansion

The church experienced significant growth as more individuals and families joined our community. We launched various ministries including Sunday School, Youth Church, and Men's and Women's Fellowships to cater to all age groups and life stages.

2020-2023
Building Community

Despite global challenges, CLC Berlin remained steadfast in its mission. We strengthened our community bonds, established regular prayer meetings, and deepened our commitment to discipleship and spiritual growth.

2024-Present
Expanding Our Reach

Today, CLC Berlin continues to grow with a vision to plant new churches across Germany and establish a Helping Centre in Kenya. We remain committed to our mission of making disciples and spreading the Gospel to all nations.
